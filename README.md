# 🐱 Cat Rush!

A fast-paced, browser-based chibi cat racing game. Help your cat dash across the
room, collect treats to open the portal, and rush through all three levels to
land on the leaderboard!

**▶️ Play now:** https://catrushgame.netlify.app

## 🎮 Gameplay

You control a chibi cat racing toward a portal at the top of the screen. Collect
enough treats to open the portal, then reach it before the timer runs out — all
while dodging obstacles. Clear all three levels to complete the game and submit
your score.

- 🐟 **Treats** — collect the required number to open the level's portal
- 🪙 **Coins** — worth points and saved toward future rewards
- 🛡️ **Shields** — absorb a single obstacle hit (10s duration)
- ⚡ **Combo** — grab 3+ items quickly for a 1.5x score multiplier

## 🕹️ Controls

- **Move:** Arrow Keys or WASD
- **Touch devices:** on-screen virtual joystick
- **Pause:** the Pause button

## 🗺️ Levels

| Level | Time | Treats to open portal | Speed | Obstacles |
|:-----:|:----:|:---------------------:|:-----:|-----------|
| 1 | 60s | 5 | 1.0x | small |
| 2 | 60s | 8 | 1.3x | small |
| 3 | 60s | 10 | 1.6x | small + medium |

You start with **3 lives** shared across all levels, and one running score for
the whole run.

## 🏆 Scoring & Leaderboard

Score comes from collectibles, the combo multiplier, a level-completion bonus,
and a time bonus. You can only submit to the leaderboard by reaching **Game
Clear** (finishing all 3 levels). See [SCORING.md](SCORING.md) for the full
breakdown.
